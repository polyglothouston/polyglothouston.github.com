Polyglot Programmers of Houston

We are a group of programmers who get together a few times a month to learn, teach, listen, and work on a wide range of programming projects. There are no rules except to show up with the enthusiasm to learn, willingness to teach, and courage to show off your ideas.

Where/When

We currently try meet every other Thursday at 6:30 PM. We are exploring different locations for meetings. Currently we meet at The Stag's Head, which is a great pub featuring decent wifi, good beers, and solid pub cuisine.

Join

Join by watching this page for meeting times and coming out to a meeting! All you need is a laptop and an idea or questions about something you want to learn.

We use Polyglot Houston Google Group for most of our discussion and meeting announcements. Open an issue if your interested in getting access, etc.

Our group has a diverse background. We have experience in medical software, all facts of web development, oil and gas software, scientific computing, server configuration, and a lot more. So, if we can't help with your questions we probably know someone who can.

Don't be shy! Just come out, bring some ideas and be ready to talk about your passion!

Contact

Check out our GitHub Profile for more information and projects we are hacking on at the moment. Feel free to open an issue up and ask a question or fork a project and show us something cool.

Who

Just a bunch of programmers who want to build a community in the Houston area that fosters learning and working on fun side-projects.
